== Description Autolab Page

In this program we will determine if any given lifestyle is feasible for a college student. Our goal is to tell the computer how luxuriously you like to eat, how much time you want to spend sleeping and relaxing, and how much you want to drink and have it tell you if you can afford that lifestyle. To complete the result you will also have to provide you current pay rate and how classes you are taking. The program wil determine if there is enough time for you complete all your coursework and relax with time left to work enough hours to afford you food and drink.
